Built the way you wish kids' apps were built

Most "free" learning apps are attention businesses. This one was built by a parent for his own five-year-old — and it shows.

Adapts like a good teacher

Every activity is chosen from your child's actual mastery — harder when they're soaring, gentler when they're stuck — and a wrong answer is never punished, only taught.

Healthy by design

15 minutes per sitting, built in. A visible countdown, a gentle two-minute warning, then a friendly "time for a break" — no negotiating with a tablet. You can grant more from the parents' area anytime.

Private. Actually private.

No ads, no tracking, no in-app purchases, no strangers' videos. Your child's progress belongs to your family — export it anytime with one tap.

Twenty little worlds, one growing garden

Core skills and the things big-box apps never teach — kindness, manners, yoga, your culture. Every success grows a plant in your child's garden.
