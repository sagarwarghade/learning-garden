Privacy Policy

Effective July 3, 2026 · Learning Garden is built by one parent in Toronto, Canada

The short version: no ads, no tracking, no analytics, no data sales — ever. Your child's learning data belongs to your family and stays on your family's device.

What the app collects from children

Nothing is transmitted to us. The Learning Garden app asks for a child's first name only so it can greet them, and records their learning progress (levels, stars, play time) so lessons can adapt. All of it is stored locally — in your browser or on your family's own device. It is never sent to our servers, because during the beta there are no servers. There are no accounts, no photos, no location, no microphone recordings, no contact information collected from children, and no third-party services inside the app.

What we collect from parents (waitlist only)

If you join the waitlist, we collect what you type into the form: your name, email address, your child's age, and anything you tell us — via Google Forms, which stores it under Google's own privacy policy. We use it for exactly one thing: sending you your invite and product updates you asked for. We never sell or share it. Email sagar.warghade.can@gmail.com and we will delete your entry within 7 days.

Children's privacy (COPPA / PIPEDA)

  • The app is used by children under parental supervision, but we do not collect, store, or transmit personal information from children.
  • A child's first name and progress never leave the family's device.
  • Parents can erase everything at any time: Grown-ups → "Reset everything", or simply clear the browser's site data.
  • Parents can export a full copy of their child's data with one tap (Grown-ups → Export progress).

Cookies & tracking

The app and this website set no advertising or analytics cookies. The app uses the browser's local storage solely to save your child's progress on your device.

If this policy changes

If we ever add features that change what data is handled (for example, optional cloud sync between your own devices), we will update this page, move the date above, and email waitlist members before anything takes effect. The promises in the short version at the top are permanent.
